Comparing the user journey for survey (A) and (B).

In this test you will be comparing one approach to the user journey against another, there are two survey's to complete in the boxes below survey (A) and (B). Each test will be conducted seperatly, at the end of each journey you will be asked questions on how you felt the journey went. After completing Survey A and B we will then ask you some questions about both journey's comparing one against the other.

When you enter the test a pop up screen will describe the actions that need to be taken, once the test has started the popup box will minimise into a circle on the left side of the test and can be pulled back up at anytime to refere to, you will be prompted throughout the journey with break out screens asking you to take specific actions, please follow these instructions until you see a screen that will let you know you have finished you will then need to click finished on the circle in the bottom left corner of the screen.
