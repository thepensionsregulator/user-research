Scheme return

Thank you for agreeing to take part in this study.

The Pensions Regulator is looking at different ways we can structure the new scheme return currently being built. This study is part of that investigation.

We’ve created two prototypes (test versions) of the new scheme return with different styles of navigation. We’d like you to enter some information into each one as if you were completing a scheme return normally.

Afterwards we’ll ask you questions about what you thought of the two versions. There are no right or wrong answers and we appreciate any feedback you may have.
