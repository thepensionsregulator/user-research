Lets get started

Context

Imagine you have previously started to complete your scheme return for this year and have already filled in some of the sections of the form. Now you’re returning to complete the remaining sections and then you will review and submit the scheme return.

Instructions

  • Fill in the remaining sections of your scheme return in sequential order (top to bottom) using the progress indicators to work out where to start
  • There will be an on-screen prompt to take a break halfway through
  • When you return, continue to complete your scheme return
  • When you reach the ‘Review and submit’ section, scan the information but do not make any changes. Submit it as if all the information is correct
  • Once you are on the ‘Scheme return complete’ page, read through the information provided then select the ‘Return to scheme options’ button.
