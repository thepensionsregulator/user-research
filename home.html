Getting started

Welcome to the SSRA Portals Design and User Research Centre.

The SSRA Portals Design and User Research Centre provides information, guidance, templates and research on the current SSRA Portals projects.

At The Pensions Regulator, our design system is based on the Government Digital Service (GDS) Design System. Referring to their resources is always a good place to start when designing for SSRA Portals.
