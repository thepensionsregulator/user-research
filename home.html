Getting started

Welcome to the SSRA Portals design and user reserach center.

The SSRA Portals design and user research center provides information, guidance, templates and research on the current SSRA Portals projects.

At The Pensions Regulator our design system is based on the the Governement Digital Design System and refering to their resources is always a good place to start when designing for SSRA Portals.
