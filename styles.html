Styles

SSRA Portals products follow The Pensions Regulators styles

The Pensions Regulator is currently in the process of building a digital design system but in the mean time you can find styles for SSRA Portals here

You should make your service look and feel like The Pensions Regulator. If you need to apply styles manually, you should still follow existing TPR conventions. For example, do not assign new meanings to colours, do not change the style of buttons or adjust the thickness of borders on form inputs.
