Progress Indicators

Adding progress indicators to sections to inform the user of when it will save

Progress Indicators Prototoype

We are conducting this research based on feedback that came out of the A/B User Journey (task list) research. The users prefered the question by question approach of filling in the forms but they were not aware their questions weren't saving until the end of the section of questions.

Although the ideal journey is to save each question and have an 'in progress' indication on the task list we are restricted by TPRs database (registary) as it is not able to save in this way.

During the A/B User Journey (task list) research we had feedback from users that progress indication within these sections would help and be a good idea so we have decided to conduct more research in this area as we would like to deliver as close to the preferred user journey as possible within our limitations.

What are we looking for

  1. Is it obvious when your work will save?
  2. Do the progress indicator help?
